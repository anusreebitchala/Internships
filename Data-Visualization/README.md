
# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Problem
The retail business lacked clear visibility into sales performance, customer contribution, and market demand due to inconsistent transactional data.

## Solution
- Cleaned data using Power Query by removing invalid quantities and unit prices.
- Created a Revenue metric using DAX.
- Built Power BI dashboards for:
  - Monthly Revenue Trend
  - Top 10 Countries by Revenue
  - Top 10 Customers by Revenue
  - Country-wise Demand

## Outcome
- Identified seasonal sales trends.
- Discovered high-value customers and top-performing countries.
- Provided insights for customer retention, inventory planning, and regional expansion.
