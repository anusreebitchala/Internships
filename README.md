# Retail Sales Performance Analysis

## Problem

The retail business lacked clear visibility into sales performance, customer contribution, and market demand due to inconsistent transactional data, making it difficult for executives to make informed business decisions.

## Solution

* Cleaned the dataset by removing invalid records (negative quantities and unit prices) using Power Query.
* Created a revenue metric and built interactive Power BI dashboards to analyze:

  * Monthly revenue trends (2011)
  * Top 10 countries by revenue (excluding the UK)
  * Top 10 customers by revenue
  * Country-wise product demand

## Outcome

* Identified seasonal sales trends and peak revenue months.
* Discovered high-value customers and top-performing markets.
* Provided actionable insights to support customer retention, inventory planning, and regional expansion strategies.
